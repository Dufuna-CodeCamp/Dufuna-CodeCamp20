"Apple iPhone 8 Plus smartphone was launched in September 2017. The phone comes with a 5.50-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1080x1920 pixels. Apple iPhone 8 Plus based on iOS 11 and packs 64GB of inbuilt storage. The Apple iPhone 8 Plus is a single SIM (GSM) smartphone that accepts a Nano-SIM card.
